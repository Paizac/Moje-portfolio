About me

Hello, I'm Paweł. I'm a beginner web developer with a huge passion for creating clean, interactive, and aesthetically pleasing websites. My adventure with code began with simple curiosity, which quickly turned into a desire to build things that work and look good.

I am drawn to the problem-solving process – from creating the perfect layout in CSS to future challenges with logic in JavaScript. I treat every project as an opportunity to learn something new and improve my skills. I believe that the key to success is continuous development and attention to detail.

I am currently focusing on mastering the fundamentals of front-end development – HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My goal is to become a versatile developer who can translate great design into functional, efficient code. If you are looking for someone with a passion for learning and a positive attitude, I would love to chat!
